--- KNOWLEDGE BASE: FS25 GRASS, HAY, & SILAGE WORKFLOWS ---
Grass is a unique crop that requires specific machinery workflows:
* Growth Cycles: Freshly seeded grass takes 4 growth stages to mature. However, because grass regrows after mowing, all subsequent regrowth cycles only take 3 stages.
* Hay Workflow: After mowing, the grass MUST be turned with a Tedder to dry it into Hay, then windrowed and baled.
* Silage Workflow: After mowing, either windrow and bale the grass (ensuring you use a Bale Wrapper to ferment it), OR use a Forage Wagon to pick up the loose grass and dump it into a Bunker Silo for compacting and covering.
